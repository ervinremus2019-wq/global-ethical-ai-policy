## Step 2: Submit to ILO
1. **Via e-Sourcing Platform:**
   - Go to [ILO e-Sourcing](https://www.ilo.org/resource/vacancy-notice/call-proposals-5).
   - Select "Policy Submission" → "International Labour Conference 2026".
   - Upload files and sign the **NDA** (required for corporations).

2. **Email Alternative:**
   - Send to: **dg@ilo.org** and **lusaka@ilo.org**
   - Subject: "Submission: Global Ethical AI Policy Framework – UN/ILO 2026"

## Step 3: Follow Up
- **Track Progress:** Use the [ILO Submission Portal](https://www.ilo.org/resource/news).
- **Media Outreach:** Share via [#HumansFirstTech](https://humansfirst.tech).
