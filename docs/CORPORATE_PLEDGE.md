## Pledge
We, **[Corporation Name]**, commit to:

1. **Human-Centric Automation:**
   - Deploy AI/robotics **only** to assist workers, not replace jobs.
   - Ban fully automated facilities unless operating alongside human-led production.

2. **Job Transition Guarantees:**
   - Reassign or retrain all workers affected by automation.
   - Maintain income/benefits during transitions.

3. **Transparency & Oversight:**
   - Disclose automation plans to the **Transnational Automation Ethics Board (TAEB)**.
   - Accept audits and penalties for non-compliance.

4. **Financial Contributions:**
   - Pay the **1–3% Automation Tax** to fund global reskilling.
   - Reinvest **20% of automation gains** into worker benefits.
