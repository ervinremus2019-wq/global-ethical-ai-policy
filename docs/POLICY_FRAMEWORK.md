## Preamble
Recognizing the urgent need for a **binding global treaty** to govern AI and automation in the workplace, this framework ensures:
1. **Human Primacy:** AI/robotics augment (not replace) human labor.
2. **Job Security:** One-to-one transition guarantees for displaced workers.
3. **Corporate Accountability:** Mandatory NDA, automation tax, and oversight.

---
## Article 1: One-to-One Job Transition Mandate
- **Reassignment:** Workers displaced by automation must be offered **comparable or superior roles**.
- **Retraining:** Fully funded upskilling in AI collaboration, robotics maintenance, etc.
- **Income Guarantee:** Wage/benefit parity during transitions.

## Article 2: Global Governance
- **Transnational Automation Ethics Board (TAEB):**
  - 50% worker representatives.
  - Powers to audit corporations and revoke automation permits.
- **Public Transparency:** Corporations must disclose automation plans **24 months in advance**.

## Article 3: Economic Protections
- **Automation Tax:** 1–3% levy on corporate AI profits, funding:
  - Universal reskilling programs.
  - Social safety nets (e.g., UBI pilots).
- **Profit Redistribution:** 20% of automation-driven gains reinvested in worker benefits.

## Article 4: Corporate Compliance
- **NDA Requirement:** Corporations must sign [NDA](../legal/NDA.md) for access.
- **Penalties:** Fines up to 5% of revenue for violations; criminal liability for executives.
